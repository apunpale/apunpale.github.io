About Pinky

I am Pinky, a Personal Stylist and Image Consultant based in London. Having personally experienced the profound ways life's transitions shape both our physical bodies and our mindset, I've developed a deep commitment to guiding women through these changes. When I had my baby, I held onto the clothes from pre-baby body for 5 years hoping, thinking that one day I will go back to that shape, size and style. And every day I wanted to change myself to the past rather than embrace my today and the demands of what I needed today. I felt guilty of letting go. And then I got an opportunity to change my outlook and show up at work with intention. So I learnt when you dress up with intention, you are confident. I want to help you break free from old style narratives and fully embrace the woman you are today. Let me reassure you one thing: I will not judge you for your body, your budget, or your existing wardrobe. I have been there done that, have had multiple times in life where budget constraints, work constraints or body feel made me struggle to invest in myself and that’s totally fine.
