About Me

My Journey: From Playing Dress-Up to Power Dressing with Purpose

For as long as I can remember, clothes have been my favourite form of creativity — a colourful canvas of confidence.
As a little girl, I loved the freedom of fashion — twirling in pretty pink dresses one day and strutting in bold trousers the next. I was playful yet purposeful, always experimenting with how clothes could change how I felt. I was the one friends turned to with curious smiles — “Where did you find that?” — because even then, I was chasing not just style, but self-expression.

My fascination with fashion grew as I did. At university and early in my career, I learned how colour could shape mood — a lesson from my dad, who taught me about “the colour of the day.” I followed it religiously, discovering how a pop of pink or a splash of sunshine yellow could shift my energy and elevate my presence.
My wardrobe was my happy place — a blend of play, power, and personality.

Then life — and motherhood — hit pause on my palette.

My wardrobe hung silent while I reached for comfort and convenience. My body had changed, my confidence faded, and getting dressed became more about survival than self-expression. The mirror reflected a woman who was giving everything to everyone — except herself.

One day, something clicked. I realised I didn’t want to disappear behind “easy.” I wanted to feel like me again — vibrant, visible, alive.

So, I began the slow, soulful journey back: learning how to dress with intention, curating colours that sparked joy, and rediscovering the power of presence.

Each outfit became a small act of self-love — a promise to honour the woman I was becoming.

As I shared this transformation with friends and family, they began asking for help — and I found my purpose.
Helping women reconnect with their reflection became my passion. I saw how many of us were hiding behind our hangers, dressing for others, not ourselves. I wanted to change that — to help women reclaim their confidence, their colour, their pink power.

So, I built my practice around wardrobe consultations and personal styling, focusing on helping women find their voice through their clothes.
Later, my journey evolved again — working with female founders and changemakers who were representing both themselves and their brands. These were powerful women with powerful stories — they just needed their style to speak the same language. Together, we worked on aligning their outer image with their inner brilliance.

Because here’s what I believe —

  • ✨ Style is a language- and women deserve to speak it boldly
  • ✨ Colour is confidence made visible

My Mission is Simple: To help you show up in your full, feminine, fearless self.

Whether you're leading a boardroom, commanding a stage, or simply rediscovering your own spark, your wardrobe should be your mirror, your armour, and your amplifier.
